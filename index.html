Where am I at?

Currently, I am in my third year at the University of Wisconsin-La Crosse moving toward a Bachelor's degree in computer science. Some of my favorite classes have been Simulation, Artificial Intelligence, and Web Development My minor is Art where I primarily focus on digital design and drawing classes.

I also love to keep active outside of the classroom! I am heavily involved in the 'CODERS' and 'Students for Sustainability' clubs on campus. These provide great outlets for community outreach as well as opportunities to meet fellow students in other departments. My love for sports has definitely not left me either. You can always finding me playing in at least one intramural or preparing for an upcoming race!
